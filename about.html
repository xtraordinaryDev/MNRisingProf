About Us

Market Twin Cities is a dynamic organization dedicated to empowering Minnesota’s rising professionals. Our mission is to connect individuals from all industries, providing them with the tools, insights, and networks needed to advance their careers and drive meaningful change in their fields. We believe that fostering collaboration, growth, and innovation is essential for the future of Minnesota’s workforce. Through our programs, events, and the annual MN Rising Professionals Conference, we create opportunities for young leaders to engage with one another, learn from industry experts, and build lasting professional relationships. At Market Twin Cities, we are committed to cultivating a vibrant network of emerging professionals who are ready to lead with impact, shape the future of their industries, and contribute to the continued success of Minnesota’s economy.
