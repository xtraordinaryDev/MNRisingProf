About Us

MN Rising Professionals was founded to address the need for a dedicated space where young professionals could connect, grow, and thrive. Recognizing a lack of professional events in Minnesota specifically tailored to rising talent—and the opportunity to create something beyond the traditional, industry-focused gatherings—the vision was to build a platform that brings together professionals from all fields for meaningful collaboration and networking.

Unlike typical professional events, MN Rising Professionals fosters connection and inspiration across all industries. Our mission is to empower and connect Minnesota rising professionals from all industries, providing them with the tools, insights, and networks needed to advance their careers and drive meaningful change within their fields.
