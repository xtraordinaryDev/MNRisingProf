MN Rising Professionals Conference 2025

The MN Rising Professionals Conference is kicking off in August 2025, marking the start of a bold new tradition for rising professionals. This inaugural three-day event will bring together 1,000 ambitious young professionals, industry experts, and community leaders for a weekend packed with opportunities to connect, grow, and be inspired. The conference will open with an electrifying night featuring a welcome introduction, an impactful keynote address, and a vibrant reception party. Saturday will deliver a full day of engaging panels, hands-on interactive sessions, open networking, and thought-provoking discussions, capped off with an evening reception perfect for building connections. The event will conclude on Sunday with empowering final remarks and actionable insights designed to help attendees elevate their careers and expand their networks. Don’t miss your chance to be part of this groundbreaking experience—more details coming soon!
